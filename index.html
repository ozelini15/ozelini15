The soloist of the group "Nerves" and already a classic of sensual rock. “Coffee is my friend, music is my precious” - you know, don't you agree?"

EDUCATION

2009-2011

Zhenya Milkovsky decided to present to the general public the first compositions - "Yes or" and "Station" Fog "". Soon the musician and vocalist released the album "In my room", and then Milkovsky's project has already turned into the group "Nerves". In the spring of 2011, the guy presented his fans with three more videos - for the singles "Silly", "Smoke" and "Coffee is my friend".

2016-2017

The group "Nerves" released two discs, united by the name "The most expensive". The main song was the composition "The most dear person". And in March 2018, the guys went on a large-scale tour of Russia and the CIS countries - the tour is dedicated to these albums.

2019-2021

In 2020, the musicians presented a concert video, which was named after the album "Sleep and Depression". In 2021, the Ukrainian rock group Nervy, popular in Russia and throughout the CIS, delighted fans with a new album 7. It consists of 21 songs.
